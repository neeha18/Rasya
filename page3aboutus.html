ABOUT US:

This website has come into picture by observing many youngsters and working professionals making unhealthy choices of eating outside food and processed foods. As they struggle with cooking because of several reasons like laziness, lack of time and may be they don't know to cook, which is affecting their health eventually in the long run. The main goal of this website is to encourage people to have a healthy lifestyle by preparing home cooked food.

Our recipes are straightforward to replicate at home and we always use easily-accessible ingredients. We provide wide variety of recipes depending upon your diet preferences, which makes you easy to choose a recipe. There are many recipe suggestions based on the persons illness or medical conditions. There are also many low calorie diet recipes for people to meet their fitness goals by not compromising on the taste.

We created this website to provide people with delicious and easy recipes to make cooking as a part of the healthy lifestyle. You can choose from a wide range of cuisines from Indian, Mexican, Thai to Japanese and nutritional food, all of our recipes are available on our website, based on your own preferences. To build confidence in the kitchen for people who can't cook, we have recipes that can be prepared with basic skills.

Cooking daily just like any other activity becomes a habit with time, which need motivation to make it enjoyable. For which we provide uninterrupted cooking you can opt for commands for handsfree cooking. We have over 500 videos on Youtube of our recipes.

The main focused users are the students living away from family, bachelors, married couples, married with kids, working professionals, to make their cooking quick, easy and healthy.

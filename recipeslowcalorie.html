SUMMER FISH STEW

This is a simplified version of the great bouillabaisse from the south of France. The amount of seafood obviously depends on the fish available in your area, and the success of the day’s catch, but the aim is to use what has been caught. The varieties will not diminish the aim or enjoyment of this dish – and even if you only have a couple of varieties this dish is still worth cooking.

SWEET POTATO & CAULIFLOWER LENTIL BOWL

Cauliflower is one of the simplest vegetables to prepare when in a hurry, as it only takes about 20 minutes to roast, and cooking it in a hot oven unlocks its earthy sweetness. This bowl features powerful complements to that sweetness: lentils, cilantro, and sweet potato hummus.

Aside from being utterly delicious, the sweet potato hummus in this bowl is a great way to increase your vegetable intake! Also, there are no chickpeas in this hummus.
